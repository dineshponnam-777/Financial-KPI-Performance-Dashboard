📊 Financial KPI Monitoring & Profitability Dashboard

🔍 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of financial performance, focusing on revenue, profitability, cost efficiency, and growth trends. It enables data-driven decision-making by highlighting key business drivers and performance gaps.

---

🎯 Key Objectives

- Monitor overall financial performance
- Analyze profitability across categories and regions
- Evaluate cost efficiency and budget performance
- Identify growth drivers and declining trends

---

📊 Key KPIs

- Total Revenue: $15M
- Net Profit: $4M
- Cost Ratio: 51%
- Budget Variance: +607K
- COGS: $8M

---

📈 Dashboard Insights

- Strong profitability with consistent revenue generation
- High cost ratio indicates opportunity for cost optimization
- Electronics category and online channel drive business growth
- Balanced regional revenue with underperformance in East region
- Declining revenue and profit trend after March requires attention

---

🛠 Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Microsoft Excel

---

🚀 Key Features

- Interactive KPI Cards
- Dynamic Visualizations (Bar, Line, Donut Charts)
- Financial Trend Analysis
- Category & Region Performance Breakdown

---

📌 Conclusion

The dashboard demonstrates strong financial performance with clear visibility into profitability and cost structure, enabling strategic business decisions.
